Culture

One of the main goals of TAI is to enhance the cultural interactions among its members. This is to preserve Telugu culture and make sure that our children do not forget the roots of where we came from and how we live.

Culture

Vanabhojanalu (Garden or Forest feast) is a kind of picnic. Friends and families gather and go on picnic to somewhere near nice and quieter place outside a city or a town usually in woods, forests or at picnic spot and spend whole day there. They prepare foods, sing songs, play games and enjoy the prepared foods.

Our Organization

The Telugu Association of Indiana is a non-profit incorporated organization of people of Telugu Origin residing in state of Indiana. It was formed in 2016 for the sole purpose of helping Telugu people living in Indiana
